Foundations of Game Design with Operational Logics

Surveys history, technology, narrative, ethics, and design of interactive computer games. Work in teams to develop novel game-design storyboards. Intended as a generally accessible undergraduate course in which students can explore the interplay of narrative, graphics, rule systems, and artificial intelligence in the creation of interactive games. Programming experience not required. (General Education Code(s): T2-Natural Sciences, IM - Interpreting Arts and Media) N. Wardrip-Fruin, J. Whitehead
