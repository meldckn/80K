Final Projects

Final projects will be your primary focus (for this class) from your section meeting week 8 until the end of week 10.

Your final project may be either a continuation of one of your earlier game projects (extending, refining, or even radically revising it) or an essay. Game projects and essays may be pursued in pairs or individually. If working in a pair, you may choose your own partner, who need not be anyone you have worked with earlier in the class. But you must declare whether you are working on a game or essay, and solo or with a partner, by the end of week 8.

In exceptional circumstances, it is possible to create a final game project that is not a continuation of one of your earlier projects, and that does not necessarily use one of the tools employed in this class. However, it is necessary for a successful final game to be already started before Week 8, and for the person/people creating it to already be familiar with the appropriate tool. So students wishing to pursue this option must show a prototype of their project, in the tool they plan to use, and receive explicit permission from their TA, at or before their section meeting Week 8.

More information about final projects will be available later in the quarter.
